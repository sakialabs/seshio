## 1. Gamification Philosophy

Seshio does not gamify behavior.

That means:
- No streaks
- No XP
- No leaderboards
- No urgency loops
- No artificial rewards

Playfulness in Seshio should feel like:
- Solving a puzzle
- Connecting ideas
- Noticing progress quietly
- Having something *click*
